Course Overview:

The final ccourse focuses on the practical use of AI in various industries, including computer vision, robotics, and finance. Students are tasked with a capstone project, where they apply their knowledge to a real-world problem. This involves defining a problem, collecting and preprocessing data, developing a model, evaluating its performance, and presenting their findings in a comprehensive report.



Instructions:

You will need to write your code in PyCharm, Visual Studio Code (needs to be installed on desktop), or other online tools like Programiz.
1. The first step is to watch the video "(AI) Practics and Projects - Full Course";

3. At the end, you will check your knowledge about (AI) Practics and Projects by taking an exam. If you pass it, you will be able to redeem your certificate.

Good luck!
